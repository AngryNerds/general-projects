Angry Nerds Studios

So, what is WaffleCode?

  • A new Java IDE from Angry Nerds Studios.
  • Lots of great new features.
  • But the biggest one is simultaneous, collaborative coding.

Collaborative Coding

  • Lets you and your team program the same project at the same time.
  • Edits made by everyone else are constantly sent to your computer.
